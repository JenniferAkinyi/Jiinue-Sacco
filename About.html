Welcome to Jiinue

Next Generation Online Banking!

Jiinue Youth Sacco is a sacco founded in Mathari Sub-county for the youths between 18-35 years old. The youths are able to join the sacco by paying a non-refundable registration fee of 1000 shillings and a minimum of 500 shillings savings a month. After six months they are able to borrow a loan of different types that is emergency, short, normal or development loan. The loans are of different interest rates per month. The loans also have different repayment periods. Loans kept in the bank as fixed deposit acquire an interest rate of 0.6% per month. To borrow a loan, the member must have three guarantors.
